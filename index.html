xG Interactive Field

xG is a measure based in the probability of a given shot will resulting in a goal; the better the xG, the closer to 100, the better the chance is.

An exceptional striker would be one who has a positive G-xG diferential meaning he finishes better than the average strike.
It's worth noting that sometimes strikers will have a big xG and at the same time a low G-xG diferential meaning that
the striker positions himself properly and manages to convert those chances.
